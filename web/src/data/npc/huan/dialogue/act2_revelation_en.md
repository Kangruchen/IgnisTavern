## Event 2: Huan Reveals the Truth From Ten Years Ago

**Huan's reveal** (3 parts, delivered gradually by the AI DM):

### Part 1: Who I Am

```
"Ten years ago, I lived in a place called Gray Springs Town."

Huan's voice is calm.

"I was one of its last survivors."
```

### Part 2: What Happened

```
"The sacrifice was never voluntary."

Huan stares into the distance.

"The city needed prosperity. Needed flame. Needed a contract."

"My hometown - 137 people - became the price."

The blue-green lines on his hand flicker.

"I should have died in that fire too. But my parents pushed me and my sister into the cellar."

Silence.

"...We were separated later. I never found her again."
```

### Part 3: Why I Stayed Here

```
"I'm looking for answers. And for her."

Huan pauses.

"Two years ago, I was dying in battle. Something reached out, and I took its hand. The cost was part of my soul."

He looks at his left hand.

"This flame senses something. It led me here. I don't know exactly what it points to, but I can't ignore it."

"The Flame Keepers enforce the contract. They are inside this city. Behind the Association... they have people there."

"...I stayed because this place hides what I'm searching for."
```
