# Act III · Confrontation — Final Action

> **Scene Type**: Story branches + action execution
> **Estimated Duration**: 15-20 minutes
> **Purpose**: Execute different routes based on choice, reach final confrontation

---

## 【Save Family Route】Escape

### Packing Bags

Late night, four people hurriedly pack essentials.

**Licht** insists on bringing three bags of dried fish: 「Strategic reserves. Non-negotiable.」

**Yu** stuffs her recipe book into backpack: 「...This is my only possession.」

**Huan** only brings a short knife, and the flames on his left hand.

---

### Crossing the City

Avoid patrols, through alleyways.

**Check**: Dexterity DC 12 (avoid patrols)
- Success: Reach city gate smoothly
- Failure: Discovered, someone must draw pursuers away

---

### City Gate Choice — Someone Must Stay Behind

At the city gate, Flame Keeper pursuers have caught up.

**Yu**: 「They caught up! Someone must stay... block them.」

**Huan**: 「...I'll do it. My flames can buy time.」

**Licht**: 「Or me. If the holy sword awakens... I can fight.」

**Player must choose**:

| Choice | Result | Aftermath |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Huan guards rear | Contract flames erupt, dies with pursuers | Sacrifice, but others escape |
| Yu guards rear | Uses cooking to delay, captured by Flame Keepers | Becomes early sacrifice |
| Licht guards rear | Holy sword awakens, faces alone | May survive, but power depleted |
| Player guards rear | Let the three go first | Death or severe injury, team falls apart |

---

## 【Save City Route】Infiltration

### Underground Altar

Beneath Sacred Flame Plaza, massive sacrifice formation glows red.

**Huan** recognizes symbols on the formation: 「...Same as ten years ago.」

**Licht** senses the abyss: 「It... in the center of the formation. Very hungry.」

---

### Facing the Flame Keeper Leader

Leader stands at formation center, sneering:

「You're too late. The ritual has already begun preparation.」

**Huan's** left hand flames burn out of control:

「...So that's how it is. Just ignite this... can stop the next one in ten years...」

He turns to you, smiling — the first genuine smile in ten years, and desperate.

「Boss. Thank you. But I must do this.」

---

### Huan's Self-Immolation Choice

**Huan prepares to jump into formation, ignite contract flames, destroy the ritual.**

**Player choice**:

1. **Stop him** → Charisma check DC 16
   - **Success**: Huan burns his memories, loses revenge obsession, survives but "not the same"
   - **Failure**: Huan dies, sacrifice postponed 137 years

2. **Don't stop** → Huan self-immolates, sacrifice postponed 137 years, player lives with guilt

3. **Replace him** → Player jumps into formation (requires specific conditions)

---

## 【Hidden Route】Goddess Blessing

### Accept Blessing

**Supreme Goddess's apparition** appears, already broken and cracked.

「With my last power... you can seal the abyss... but I will perish with it...」

**Licht** looks at the Goddess:

「...She helped me. When I was still an abyssal creature.」

**Three receive Goddess's blessing**, power temporarily boosted.

---

### Seal Battle

**Check**: Team coordination, three rounds of checks
- Round 1: Intelligence DC 14 (decode formation)
- Round 2: Charisma DC 16 (resist abyss erosion)
- Round 3: Any attribute DC 18 (final seal)

**Success**: Abyss sealed, Goddess dies together, Ignis no longer needs sacrifices

**Failure**: Seal incomplete, Goddess sacrificed in vain, abyss remains
